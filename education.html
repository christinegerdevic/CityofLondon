Education

Learning in London

London elementary and secondary schools are under the control of four school boards:

  • Thames Valley District School Board
  • London District Catholic School Board
  • Conseil scolaire Viamonde
  • Conseil scolaire catholique Providence

London has two post-secondary facilities:

  • Fanshawe College
  • Western University

Western University also has three affiliated University Colleges:

  • Brescia University College
  • Huron University College
  • King's University College

More Info

Continuing education is an important component of both Western and Fanshawe programs and encompasses business, the arts and a broad array of leisure activities and areas of human interest.

As well, private schooling is offered from junior kindergarten through to high school. To search for registered elementary and secondary private schools in London, please visit the Ontario Ministry of Education's website. There are also more than 20 registered private vocational schools in the city. To search for registered private career colleges in London, please visit the Service Ontario website.

The City of London also provides a wide variety of learning opportunities through its Recreational Services Department and the London Public Library.
